How do I get started with Classarium?

Simply click "Get Started" on any page, create your school account, and follow the guided setup wizard. You can import your existing data or start fresh. Most schools are up and running within a day.

Can I try Classarium before committing?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Professional plan features. No credit card required. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime during or after the trial.

Do you offer onboarding and training?

Yes! All plans include access to our documentation, video tutorials, and help center. Professional and Enterprise plans include live onboarding sessions with our customer success team.
