About Classarium

We're on a mission to transform how schools operate — making administration effortless so educators can focus on what matters most: students.

Our Mission

To empower every school with affordable, world-class management tools that simplify administration, enhance communication, and improve educational outcomes. We believe technology should reduce workload for teachers and administrators — not add to it. Classarium was built from the ground up to replace fragmented systems with one cohesive platform that works for schools of every size, from single-classroom academies to multi-campus institutions.

Our Vision

To become the backbone of education administration across Africa and beyond — a platform where every student record, every grade, every interaction, and every insight lives in one place. We envision a future where no school is held back by outdated tools, where data-driven decisions are the norm, and where technology amplifies the human connection at the heart of education.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

From a small idea to powering hundreds of schools.

2021

Founded

Classarium was born from the frustration of managing school records with spreadsheets. Two friends set out to build a better way.

2022

First 100 Schools

Reached our first major milestone — 100 schools across Nigeria adopted Classarium for their daily operations.

2023

Mobile App Launched

Released iOS and Android apps, bringing the full power of Classarium to parents, teachers, and administrators on the go.

2024

10,000 Students Served

Surpassed 10,000 active student records on the platform. Expanded to Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa.

2025

AI-Powered Features

Launched AI-driven result analytics, smart attendance prediction, and automated report card generation — setting a new standard for EdTech in Africa.
