Powerful Features

Everything your school needs to run smoothly — from academic management to communication, assessment, operations, and administration.

Academic

Academic Management

Handle every aspect of academic life — from enrollment to graduation — in one place.

Student Management

Complete student profiles with enrollment, class assignment, promotion, and academic history tracking.

Staff Management

Manage teachers, administrators, and support staff with role-based profiles and workload tracking.

Result Processing

Enter, calculate, and publish student results with automatic grading, GPAs, and position computation.

Report Cards

Generate beautiful, printable report cards with custom templates, comments, and school branding.

Communication

Stay Connected

Bridge the gap between school, teachers, parents, and students with seamless communication tools.

Real-time Chat

Instant messaging between teachers, parents, and admin with read receipts and file sharing.

Parent Portal

Dedicated portal for parents to track grades, attendance, fees, and communicate with teachers.

Announcements

Broadcast important updates to the entire school or specific groups with push notifications.

Notifications

Smart alerts for events, deadlines, low balances, and custom triggers via email, SMS, and in-app.

Assessment

Modern Assessment Tools

Go beyond paper with digital exams, auto-grading, and deep performance analytics.

CBT Exams

Conduct computer-based tests with timer, randomization, anti-cheat measures, and instant scoring.

Question Bank

Build and organize a reusable question bank by subject, topic, and difficulty level.

Auto Grading

Automatic scoring for objective questions with support for partial credit and rubric-based assessment.

Analytics

Visual dashboards showing student performance trends, class averages, and subject comparisons.

Operations

School Operations

Manage the day-to-day running of your school with powerful operational tools.

Attendance

Daily attendance tracking with biometrics support, absence reports, and parent notifications.

Timetable

Create and manage class, teacher, and room timetables with conflict detection and drag-and-drop editing.

Library

Catalog books, track issuance and returns, manage fines, and provide digital library access.

Hostel

Manage hostel allocations, room assignments, bed spaces, visitor logs, and maintenance requests.

Transport

Plan bus routes, assign drivers, track vehicles, and notify parents about pickup and drop-off times.

Medical

Track student health records, immunizations, allergies, clinic visits, and emergency contacts.

Administration

Administration & Security

Enterprise-grade tools to manage, secure, and analyze your school's data.

Multi-tenant

Manage multiple schools, branches, or campuses from a single dashboard with data isolation.

Role-Based Access (RBAC)

Define granular permissions for admins, teachers, parents, and students with custom roles.

Audit Logs

Track every action across the platform — who did what, when, and from where — for full accountability.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time insights on enrollment trends, revenue, attendance rates, and academic performance.
